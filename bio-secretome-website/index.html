About Us

A biotechnology R&D company rooted in scientific excellence

Bio Secretome SL is a biotechnology research & development company located in Madrid, Spain. We specialize in researching innovative applications of microalgae, with a primary focus on developing a proprietary Chlorella suspension designed for future use in cosmetic formulations.

Our work combines biotechnology, sustainability, and scientific excellence to create next-generation cosmetic ingredients — bridging cutting-edge life sciences with the elegance and rigor of the premium cosmetics industry.

  • Microalgae-focused research program
  • Proprietary Chlorella suspension in development
  • European science, sustainability & standards
